Picture pattern▸

Make a pattern from a picture instead of the motif — three house pictures are always here to play with. Pick the shape that tiles (Triangle, Square or Hexagon), then how the picture fits it. Tessellate repeats the picture exactly, piece by piece — so the picker is the true shape (dots stay free only for triangles, since any triangle tessellates). Stretch lets you drag the dots freely and pulls what's inside them to fill the shape. Hexagons offer Full (a whole hexagon of the photo, tessellated) or Slice (one wedge stretched and repeated kaleidoscope-style). Uploads stay saved here — remove one with its ×.

Outline▸

Draws a line on the motif (turn it on with the switch in this section’s header). Around the shape traces one clean line hugging the outside of the whole motif — it doesn’t cut into the fill or show the seams between pieces. Along each edge instead strokes every edge of every piece, right on the outline you drew with the fine-tune points.

Saved designs▸

Your work is kept in this browser automatically — close the tab and reopen to pick up exactly where you left off (only Reset goes back to the original trunk motif). “Save current design” pins a copy, named with the moment (click the ✎ to rename).
