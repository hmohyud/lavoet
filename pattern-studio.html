Shade line gives a flat, two-tone “shaded” look: it splits the motif into a lit half and a shadow half along a line you drag on the canvas. Per motif keeps one straight line across the whole motif (reads like a lit 3-D form); Per piece turns the line with each piece (a uniform faceted pinwheel).
